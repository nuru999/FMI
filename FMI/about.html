About Us

Shaping Global Leaders in Theology & Business

At FMI Biblical Studies & Seminary, we are committed to providing world-class theological education. With a presence across multiple countries, including Kenya, the United Kingdom, the United States, South Africa, Tanzania, and Zambia, our mission is to raise a new generation of leaders who are equipped both spiritually and academically.

Our programs in theology, leadership, and business cater to individuals who aspire to serve in ministry, lead organizations, and make a lasting impact on their communities. With a focus on academic excellence and spiritual growth, FMI is shaping the future of global Christian leadership.
